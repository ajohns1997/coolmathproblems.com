Today's Lesson: CUBES Math Strategy Review

The CUBES math strategy provides a fun and simple way to pick apart and understand what is being asked in a math word problem. When working with math word problems, the acronym CUBES will help you decode and work through each math word problem. Each letter of the acronym represents a different step of the strategy. Therefore, each letter of CUBES stands for an actionable item that you will do to solve your math word problems. C means to circle the numbers. U means to underline important words. B means to box the question. E means to eliminate unnecessary information. S means to solve the word problem.

Please review below the five Math word problems which display how to use each step of the CUBES math strategy. Using different types of math word problems will show you that the CUBES strategy could be used for all types of math word problems. The main purpose is to find a strategy that will help you to understand a math word problem, plan out your strategy to solve it, and recheck your answer. Using the CUBES strategy will start you to think and talk about your word problem solving strategies while building your confidence that you CAN conquer math word problems!

Then, you will find math worksheets to practice using the CUBES strategy along with creating and solving your own math word problem using the CUBES Strategy on the Practice tab.

Step 1: After you read through the word problem the first time, Circle all of the numbers or number words in the problem.
Step 2: Think about what exactly the question is asking you to do with the numbers. Then, Underline the question that is found within the Math word problem.
Step 3: Draw a box around the key words found within the Math word problem.
Step 4: Eliminate any extra information by drawing a strikethrough on the words that are extra within the Math word problem.
Step 5: After you have gone through the CUBES math strategy steps, apply the steps to Solve the Math word problem.
