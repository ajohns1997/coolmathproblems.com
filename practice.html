Practice Using the CUBES Math Strategy

Math word problems can be tricky to solve. Using the CUBES strategy can make solving math word problems easier to understand. Watching the video will remind you of the important steps to solve a math word problem using the CUBES strategy.

Please Click to Watch the Video
To really learn "how to do" word problems, you will need to practice, practice, practice. Sounds simple and easy right? Yes it is, and yes it really does work. The more time you practice, you will see your thinking process and Math strategies improve. By practicing using the CUBES strategy, you are encourage to slow down and think before and after solving the word problem.
